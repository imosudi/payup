About payUP

payUP payroll management system is the system by which an employer can manage the wages and salaries of the employees. The salary structure includes salary, allowances, deductions, and net payable to the employees.

This system can be integrated with leave and attendance tracking systems and employee self-service portal and are used to keep track of employee's working hours, calculate salaries, calculate taxes and deductions, print payslip, etc.

These complicated computations that are performed manually can be automated using payUP payroll management system.
